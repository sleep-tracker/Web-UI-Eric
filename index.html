What We Do

Not everyone needs 8 hours of sleep, but how do you know if you’re someone lucky enough to need only 6? Or an unlucky one that needs 10?! Enter Rest Well. We build the technology that analyzes the sleep patterns that you enter, along with how you feel that day.

How We Can Help

How can keeping track of your sleeping patterns help you get more rest?

Understand Your Sleep

You can understand your sleep patterns and habits. We’re often very surprised at the difference between how we think we sleep and how we’re really sleeping.

Take Action

You can start taking action. Tracking your sleep shows you what you need to change. It can also show you if sleep aids like Melatonin are working for you over the long term.

Help Your Doctor

If you decide to consult a doctor, your doctor can use your diary to find the right solution to help you sleep.

Sleep Efficiency Reports

A comprehensive report into your sleep patterns helps you understand when you should go to bed, when you should wake up, and when you sleep best.

Contact Us

Whether it's a question, concern, or a chat about how great sleep is, we promise to get back to you as soon as possible.
